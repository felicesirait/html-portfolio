BEST K-DRAMAS YOU MUST KNOW ACCORDING TO ICE

My Top 3 K-DRAMAS List:

1. Lovely Runner

This drama tells the story of a person named Ryu Sun-jae, who is a top star who has been in the spotlight since his debut. Her life seems perfect, but being in the entertainment industry has worn her down.

Meanwhile, Im Sol loves Ryu Sun-jae as an adoring fan.When he was a child, he had an accident. Because of this, she gave up on her dream, but hearing Ryu Sun-jae's song on the radio gave her comfort and she became his fan from then on.

One day, Im Sol heard the news that Ryu Sun-jae died of shock after taking sleeping pills and cold medicine together. He felt deep sadness at the tragic news. Miraculously, Im Sol somehow travels back in time 15 years. There, he confronts Ryu Sun-jae, a 19-year-old high school student. He struggles to prevent his tragic future

2. My Demon

This drama is about a person named Do Do-hee who is the successor of the Future Group. He has an arrogant and hot-headed personality, who doesn't trust anyone. He is cynical about love. Do Do-hee gets involved with a demon named Jung Gu-won and makes a contract marriage with him. He faces major changes in his life.

Jung Gu-won is a devil. He can live forever by making a dangerous but sweet deal with humans living a life in hell. He looks down on humans and he has roamed this world like an apex predator for 200 years. He got involved with Do Do-hee and somehow suddenly lost his powers. He then entered into a contract marriage with her. To prevent his own extinction, he must protect Do Do-hee who has taken all his power. Their relationship develops romantically.

3. My Man Is Cupid

This drama is about a fairy who was hit by an arrow she shot herself. He fell in love with a woman.

Cheon Sang-hyeok is a charismatic fairy, who easily makes women fall in love with him. 500 years ago, his wings burned. To restore his wings, he goes through 27 metamorphoses. On the 28th time, he got involved with Oh Baek-ryun.

Oh Baek-ryun is a veterinarian and she works at an animal clinic. She is a beautiful woman. She has made many men fall in love with her because of her charm. Every time these men had a romantic relationship with her, they always had a near-death experience and then ran away from it. Due to her bad luck, Oh Baek-ryun doesn't date anyone, but she desperately wants to meet her destined man.
